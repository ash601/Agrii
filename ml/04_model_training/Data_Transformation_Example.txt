=========================================================================================================
DATA TRANSFORMATION: BEFORE AND AFTER FEATURE ENGINEERING
=========================================================================================================

When explaining the Machine Learning pipeline, use this document to demonstrate exactly how the data 
is transformed so the AI can understand it. Because this is a plain text file, the table will maintain 
its look in any editor.

---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. THE RAW DATA (BEFORE TRANSFORMATION)
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
This is the human-readable format that we download from the CSV file. It contains text strings and 
date formats. Machine learning algorithms CANNOT perform math on this format.

STATE         | DISTRICT    | MARKET      | COMMODITY | VARIETY | GRADE | PRICE DATE | MODAL_PRICE
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Maharashtra   | Nashik      | Lasalgaon   | Onion     | Other   | FAQ   | 15/06/2023 | 1250
Rajasthan     | Chittorgarh | Nimbahera   | Wheat     | Other   | FAQ   | 06/06/2023 | 2300


---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
2. THE TRANSFORMED DATA (AFTER FEATURE ENGINEERING)
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
After the Python script runs, the rows are completely rewritten into pure numerical matrices. 
This is the exact format that gets fed into the AI algorithm:

STATE | DISTRICT | MARKET | COMMODITY | VARIETY | GRADE | DAY | MONTH | YEAR | MODAL_PRICE
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
14    | 52       | 118    | 3         | 12      | 0     | 15  | 6     | 2023 | 1250
23    | 18       | 94     | 8         | 12      | 0     | 6   | 6     | 2023 | 2300


---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
KEY CONCEPTS TO EXPLAIN:
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. Time Engineering: 
The text string "15/06/2023" has been broken apart. The AI now receives three separate numerical 
columns: 15, 6, and 2023. This is how the AI algorithm learns mathematical seasonality.

2. Label Encoding: 
The word "Maharashtra" has been permanently translated to 14, and "Onion" to 3. 

3. The Math: 
The AI algorithm (Random Forest Regressor) takes these integer inputs (14, 52, 118, 3...), multiplies 
them by complex weights inside its decision trees, and accurately predicts the target variable: 1250.
