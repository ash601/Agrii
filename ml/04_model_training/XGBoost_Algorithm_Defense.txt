=========================================================================================================
DEFENSE Q&A: ALGORITHM JUSTIFICATION (WHY XGBOOST?)
=========================================================================================================

If an examiner asks: "You used XGBoost (Extreme Gradient Boosting). Why did you choose that specific algorithm over Random Forest, Linear Regression, or a Neural Network?"

Use these 4 arguments to mathematically and logically defend your choice:

---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. IT LEARNS FROM ITS MISTAKES (Sequential Boosting)
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
"Unlike a Random Forest (which builds all its decision trees blindly at the same time), XGBoost builds its mathematical trees sequentially. Every new tree it builds specifically looks at the errors made by the previous tree and mathematically forces itself to correct them. This 'Gradient Boosting' method guarantees maximum accuracy."

---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
2. IT IS THE GLOBAL INDUSTRY STANDARD FOR TABULAR DATA
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
"While Neural Networks are great for Images and Text, they often overfit or fail on Excel-style data. XGBoost is widely recognized as the undisputed champion for tabular, structured datasets like ours. It consistently wins global Kaggle data science competitions."

---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
3. IT UNDERSTANDS NON-LINEAR RELATIONSHIPS
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
"Agricultural prices do not move in straight lines. They are highly complex and seasonal (e.g., tomatoes might be cheap in June, spike in August, and drop again in October). Standard algorithms like 'Linear Regression' fail here because they expect straight-line relationships. XGBoost excels at finding these non-linear, zig-zagging patterns."

---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
4. BUILT-IN REGULARIZATION (Prevents Memorization)
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
"XGBoost contains built-in L1 and L2 mathematical regularization. This acts as a strict penalty system that prevents the AI from simply memorizing the dataset (Overfitting). Because of this, it scores incredibly high when tested on blind, real-world data."
