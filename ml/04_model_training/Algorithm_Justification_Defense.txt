=========================================================================================================
DEFENSE Q&A: ALGORITHM JUSTIFICATION (WHY RANDOM FOREST?)
=========================================================================================================

If an examiner asks: "You used a Random Forest Regressor. Why did you choose that specific algorithm instead of Linear Regression or a Neural Network?"

Use these 4 arguments to mathematically and logically defend your choice:

---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. IT IS AN 'ENSEMBLE' METHOD (High Accuracy, Low Overfitting)
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
"A Random Forest doesn't just build one predictive model; it builds hundreds of individual 'Decision Trees' and averages their answers together. This 'wisdom of the crowd' approach prevents the AI from simply memorizing the dataset (Overfitting) and guarantees much higher real-world accuracy than a single algorithm."

---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
2. IT UNDERSTANDS NON-LINEAR RELATIONSHIPS
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
"Agricultural prices do not move in straight lines. They are highly complex and seasonal (e.g., tomatoes might be cheap in June, spike in August, and drop again in October). Standard algorithms like 'Linear Regression' fail here because they expect straight-line relationships. Random Forest excels at finding these non-linear, zig-zagging patterns."

---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
3. IT HANDLES CATEGORICAL DATA PERFECTLY
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
"Because our data is heavily categorical (States, Mandis, Crops), we had to translate words into ID numbers using Label Encoding. Random Forest is uniquely designed to process these integer IDs correctly without accidentally treating them as mathematical rankings (it knows State '14' is not greater than State '3', they are just different places)."

---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
4. IT IS ROBUST TO MARKET OUTLIERS
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
"Agricultural markets are prone to sudden, massive price spikes due to extreme weather or sudden shortages (Outliers). Many algorithms get confused and broken by outliers. Random Forest is mathematically robust against them. A sudden 500% price spike in one Mandi won't destroy the logic of the entire national model."
